Diary entry Anne Boleyn (from Henry VIII)



Dear diary,

Yesterday, Anne Boleyn was executed. She was convicted of treason, adultery and incest.
Why, well, because she was not submissive to me, she kept behaving like she was more
important than me, which of course is not the case. But the most important reason is the
following: when I was still married to Catherine of Aragon, she kept telling me ‘Divorce
Catherine of Aragon, marry me, cause Í will be the person who will be able to give you a son.
Break of your relationships with the Pope and with the Church, found your own church, because
Í will be the person who will be able to give you a son.’ But now, when I did that all, founded
the Church of England, she didn’t give me a son! Only a daughter, princess Elizabeth I. But I
don’t want a daughter, I want a male heir, someone who can succeed me as king when I die.
But she failed to do that. That’s the reason why I falsely accused her. I said she had relations
with five other men, including her brother, George Boleyn, what resulted into adultery and
incest. But treason was actually true, because she lied to me, she said she would give me a son,
but she didn’t do that, so she actually lied to me, and lying to the king is actually treason. So at
the moment Anne Boleyn was executed I felt glad, because I’m able to start another marriage
with someone who wíll be able to give me a son. I regret about our marriage that she didn’t give
me a son, but I don’t totally regret our marriage, I loved her so much more than Catherine of
Aragon.

Fun Fact: Anne Boleyn appears to be Henry VIII’s biggest love.
